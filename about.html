About Us

At PetMalu, we are passionate about improving the lives of stray animals and pet owners in the Philippines. Our platform was created to bridge the gap between those looking to adopt a pet and those needing reliable pet care services. By fostering connections and providing essential resources, we strive to make a positive impact on the community and promote responsible pet ownership.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

At PetMalu, our mission is to revolutionize pet adoption and care in the Philippines by providing a user-friendly platform that connects loving homes with stray animals and reliable pet-sitting services. We aim to enhance animal welfare, reduce euthanasia rates, and promote responsible pet ownership through education and community support.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every stray animal finds a loving home, and pet owners have access to trustworthy care resources. By building a compassionate community, we aspire to create a safe and nurturing environment for all pets, fostering lasting bonds between animals and their families.

What We Do

Adoption Services

We connect adopters with shelters and rescues to help find forever homes for pets in need.

Pet Care Services

Our platform offers reliable pet sitting, walking, and other essential care services for pet owners.
