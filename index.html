About us

At Petyaya, we are dedicated to providing reliable and trustworthy pet-sitting services across the Philippines. Our platform connects pet owners with qualified caregivers to ensure their pets are well taken care of when they need it most. By fostering a community of responsible and caring pet sitters, we strive to make pet care seamless and stress-free for pet owners.

Mission

At Petyaya, our mission is to connect pet owners with trusted and reliable pet sitters across the Philippines, ensuring pets receive excellent care while providing peace of mind to their owners. We aim to build a compassionate community that promotes responsible pet care and supports the well-being of every pet.

Vision

We envision a future where pet owners can confidently access trustworthy and reliable care for their pets. By building a strong network of compassionate and skilled pet sitters, we aspire to create a supportive community that prioritizes the well-being of pets and fosters lifelong connections between sitters, pets, and owners.
